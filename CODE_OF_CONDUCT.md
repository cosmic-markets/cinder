# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Cinder to be a professional and welcoming project. Contributors and
maintainers are expected to communicate respectfully, focus criticism on the
work, and assume good intent while still being direct about correctness and
safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, personal attacks, and repeated disruption
are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate
this code of conduct. Serious incidents can be reported privately to the project
maintainers.
